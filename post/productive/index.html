“Productivity is less about what you do with your time And more about how you run your mind”

Tips on how to be more productive:

1. Wake up early in the Morning:

Waking up early has a lot of benefits such as boosted energy levels and high concentration which is PERFECT for studies or any other work. Starting your day with a huge boost in concentration will help you focus and achieve your goals and get all your work done. Not to mention, studies show that studying between 6 AM - 11 AM is the MOST EFFECTIVE way of studying for students.

Drinking a glass of water every 1 - 2 hour is a great practice to keep your brain fuelled with energy.

2. Make a reasonable to-do list:

First of all, don’t overload yourself. Most to-do lists fail because we make them too difficult or the tasks are unbalanced. Some tasks will take a very long time, others won’t take any time. This creates an unbalance in how you divide your time. What happens then is that our to-do list becomes a hierarchy. Yes, that is right. Because then we do the easy stuff, and then become really distracted on the hard part.
